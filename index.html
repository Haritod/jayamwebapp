About Us

IT STARTED, QUITE SIMPLY, LIKE THIS...

It was in 2006, some women we met in a marriage function were lamenting that their children are not getting authentic Tambrahm Pickles and other Podis. Though Indian Stores in their neighbourhood sell these products and popular brands courier their products, the yearning for authentic food products ,that taste as good as the ones prepared by their mothers and grandmothers, remains insatiable.

From being an humble housewife, with children having grown up, I thought of spending my time more purposefully. On enquiry, my relatives and close friends whose children are living abroad, expressed that they too faced the problem. They could not prepare and dispatch to their children living abroad. Satisfied with our products the parents of NRI Children happily lapped up the entire quantity for onward dispatch.

Our products are being regularly despatched to US, Singapore, Gulf countries and Australia. Many working women in the busy Towns also prefer our products instead of mass manufactured branded stuff.

We have not spent a single pie on advertisement. By word of mouth, our products got wider recognition.

Though requests for our products poured in from different quarters, we did not scale up the quantity of our products, all these years, for want of manpower and support system.

We were taking the assistance of poor women living around us as and when needed. Presently, we are employing on regular basis, around 10 women living in the villages around Vadalur (near Neyveli in Cuddalore District) for sorting and cleaning the ingredients thoroughly. As we work with them we listen to their personal life situations. Thanks to the employment provided by us, their self confidence has increased manifold and they feel empowered with the decent wages they earn, from a villagerâ€™s perspective. This gives us immense satisfaction and a proud feeling of contributing to the society, albeit in a small way.
